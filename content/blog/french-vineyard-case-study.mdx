## A Partnership for Excellence

When a renowned French vineyard approached us about implementing OSIRRIS technology, they had two primary goals: maintain their premium wine quality while reducing environmental impact.

## The Challenge

Traditional irrigation methods in vineyards often rely on fixed schedules that don't account for varying soil conditions, weather patterns, or the specific needs of different grape varieties.

## The Solution

We deployed our smart tensiometer network across the vineyard, providing real-time data on soil moisture levels at various depths. This data, combined with our AI-powered decision support system, enabled precise irrigation management.

## Results

After one growing season:
- 40% reduction in water usage
- Improved grape quality metrics
- More consistent yields across different vineyard sections
- Reduced labor costs for irrigation management
